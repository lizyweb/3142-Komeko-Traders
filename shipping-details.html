Shipping Details

At KOMEKO TRADERS PRIVATE LIMITED, we strive to ensure that your orders are delivered to you in a timely and efficient manner. Our Shipping Policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding the processing, handling, and delivery of our products.

1. Order Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 48 hours after receiving payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

2. Shipping Rates and Methods

Shipping rates vary based on the delivery location, weight, and size of the order. Shipping charges will be calculated and displayed at checkout. We offer standard and expedited shipping options to meet your delivery needs. You can select your preferred option at checkout.

3. Estimated Delivery Time

Domestic Orders (India): Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days, depending on your location and the selected shipping method. International Orders: Delivery times vary by country and shipping method and generally range from 7-21 business days. Please note that delivery times are estimates and may vary due to unforeseen circumstances, such as weather, customs delays (for international shipments), or other logistical issues.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS, allowing you to monitor the delivery status. You can use the tracking number to check the status of your shipment through the carrier’s website.

5. Shipping Restrictions

Certain locations may have restrictions on specific products. We are unable to ship to some restricted regions and areas based on legal and logistical constraints. For international orders, the customer is responsible for understanding the import regulations and any restrictions in their country.

6. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

For international orders, any customs fees, import duties, taxes, or other charges imposed by the destination country are the customer’s responsibility. These fees are not included in the product or shipping cost. KOMEKO TRADERS PRIVATE LIMITED is not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance procedures or any additional charges incurred.

7. Address Accuracy

Please ensure that your shipping address is correct and complete at the time of order placement. Incorrect or incomplete addresses may result in delays or undeliverable orders. If you realize that your address is incorrect after placing the order, please contact us as soon as possible at info@komekotraders.in or +91 93457 68235. We will make every effort to update your address before shipment, but we cannot guarantee changes once the order has been processed.

8. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of receipt with photos of the damaged product and packaging. We will assess the situation and provide a solution, which may include a replacement or refund. For lost packages, please contact us with your order and tracking information. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure your satisfaction.
